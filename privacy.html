Verb Drill

Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Overview

Verb Drill is an offline flashcard app for learning verb conjugations. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data.

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No third-party services or SDKs
  • No network requests (fully offline)

Data Storage

The app stores only your language preferences locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to us
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the app

Children's Privacy

This app is safe for all ages. We do not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy. Check this page for the latest version.
